Architectural Wonders

Explore the beauty of architectural designs from around the world.

The Colosseum in Rome is a marvel of ancient Roman engineering and design. Built in 70-80 AD, it could hold up to 80,000 spectators and was primarily used for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

The Taj Mahal in India is a stunning example of Mughal architecture. Constructed in the 17th century, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and symbolizes love and devotion.
